Caramel Popcorn

Home

Great caramel popcorn for any holiday or occasion. If you like chewy popcorn, bake the coated corn a little less

Ingredients

  • 5 quarts popped popcorn
  • 1 cup butter
  • 2 cups brown sugar
  • ½ cup corn syrup
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon baking soda

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients, making sure your butter is softened, and your eggs are room temperature.
  2. Melt butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Stir in brown sugar, corn syrup, and salt. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly. Boil without stirring 4 minutes
  3. Remove pan from heat and stir in vanilla and baking soda. Pour caramel in a thin stream over popcorn while stirring. Stir until evenly coated.
  4. Divide popcorn between two large shallow baking dishes. Bake in the preheated oven, stirring every 15 minutes, for 1 hour.
  5. Remove from oven and let cool completely before breaking into pieces. Enjoy!
